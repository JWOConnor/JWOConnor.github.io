Want to know a little more about me?

My name is Jessica, and I'm a list making, tea drinsking, excel loving, over-zealous home cook enjoying life (and possibly a cocktail) in Baton Rouge with a cat, dog, bee hive & husband Anthony.


90% of my Pandora radio stations are build on Broadway hits, I can't single task long enough to watch a movie at the theatre and I sometime make color-coordinated, cross-referenced lists for things like my groceries.


My need for a constant and varied list of projects is something that I've only recently realized most definitely is could possibly be a genetic trait. Thanks, mom and dad, I think Christopher got it too.
